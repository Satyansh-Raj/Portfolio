About Me

Hello! I'm Satyansh Raj, a Computer Science and Engineering student at Lovely Professional University, with a keen interest in cybersecurity, digital forensics, and system-level problem solving. I've worked on a range of practical projects—including forensic investigations using Autopsy and FTK Imager, a custom-built Wi-Fi deauther for security testing, and terminal-based applications in Python and C.

My technical skills span across C++, Python, Bash, and Solidity, with a strong foundation in system administration and forensic analysis. I enjoy breaking down complex problems, uncovering hidden data, and building tools that enhance security awareness. I'm always looking to explore new technologies and contribute meaningfully to the field of cybersecurity.
